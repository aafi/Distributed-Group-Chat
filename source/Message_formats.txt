MESSAGE FORMATS:


When new client is requesting ID:

What sequencer is expecting: Request#IP#Port
What sequencer will return :
1. Client ID/"Max Limit Reached"
2. Seq#ClientInfo#Ip#Port#ClientID#Ip#Port#Client#.......................		[MULTICAST]


When client sends message:

What sequencer is expecting: Message#ClientID#MsgID#Message

What sequencer will return:
1. Seq#GlobalSeqID#ClientID#MsgID#Message 				[MULTICAST]		




Client Messages:

1. Joining a Chat:
	Send: "JOIN"
	Receive: "JOINLEADER#Leader_IPAddr#Leader_Port"
